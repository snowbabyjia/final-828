Papers (co)authored by Frans Kaashoek prior to joining MIT

For recent papers see the PDOS publications.
Partial list of journal papers:
  • FLIP: an Internetwork Protocol for Supporting Distributed Systems,
    by M. Frans Kaashoek, Robbert van Renesse, Hans van Staveren, and Andrew S. Tanenbaum.
    This appears in ACM Transactions on Computer Systems, Vol. 11, No. 1, pages 77-106, February 1993.

  • A Comparison of Two Distributed Systems: Amoeba and Sprite,
    by Douglis, F., M. F. Kaashoek, A. S. Tanenbaum, J. K. Ousterhout.
    This appears in Computing Systems,Vol. 4, No. 4, pages 353-384, 1991.

  • The Amoeba Distributed Operating System - a Status Report,
    by Tanenbaum, A.S., M.F. Kaashoek, R. van Renesse, H.E. Bal.
    This appears in Computer Communications,Vol. 14, No. 6, pages 324-335, August 1991.

  • Orca: A language for Parallel Programming of Distributed Systems,
    by Bal, H.E., M.F. Kaashoek, A.S. Tanenbaum.
    This appears in IEEE Transaction on Software Engineering, Vol. 18, No. 3, pages 190-205, March 1992.

  • Replication Techniques for Speeding up Parallel Applications on Distributed Systems,
    by Bal, H.E., M.F. Kaashoek, A.S. Tanenbaum, J. Jansen.
    This appears in Concurrency Practice & Experience,Vol. 4, No. 5, pages 337-355, August 1992.

  • Parallel Programming Using Shared Objects and Broadcasting,
    by Tanenbaum, A.S., M.F. Kaashoek, H.E. Bal.
    This appears in IEEE Computer, Vol. 25, No. 8, pages 10-19, August 1992.

  • A Comparison of Two Paradigms for Distributed Shared Memory ,
    by Levelt, W.G., M.F. Kaashoek, H.E. Bal, A.S. Tanenbaum.
    This appears in Software Practice & Experience,Vol. 22, No. 11, pages 985-1010, November 1992.

  • Group Communication in Amoeba and its Applications,
    by Kaashoek, M. F., A. S. Tanenbaum, K. Verstoep.
    This appears in Distributed Systems Engineering, vol. 1, no. 1, September 1993, pages 48-58.

    Conference papers:
  • Object Distribution in Orca using Compile-Time and Run-Time Techniques,
    by Bal, H. E., M. F. Kaashoek.
    This appears in Proceedings of OOPSLA-'93, Eight Annual Conference on Object Oriented Programming Systems, Languages, and Applications, Washington, D.C., September 26 - October 1, 1993, pages 162-177.
